ENVI  |  Grow Computer

The Technaponics ENVI® is a monolithic grow computer offering a suite of environmental sensors and real time controls accessible on your phone, tablet, or computer from anywhere in the world. The ENVI® is powerful and easy to use with a seamless plug & play experience for any garden whether it be soil, hydroponic, or aeroponic.


  • Controls & Sensors
  • HD Camera
  • 8 Programmable Outlets
  • pH, EC, Water & Leaf Temperatures
  • CO2, R.H. & Ambient Temperature
  • PAR, Lux, IR, RGB, UVA, UVB
  • Soil Moisture (2x) & Temperature (2x)
  • Reservoir Level
  • System Info
  • Made in the USA
  • 3-year warranty
  • Free software updates
  • Android, IoS, Windows Compatible
